Foodeli is an innovative and efficient system of deliveries dedicated primarily to restaurant services. The solutions offered by Foodeli, together with our lightning-fast on-demand deliveries, relieve the restaurants from the burden of managing deliveries to their clients themselves.

Foodeli replaces the need for restaurants to hire their own drivers with our efficient service, removing the organisational issues and financial obstacles for them.

By thy end of 2020, Foodeli network has handled over 1.350.000 deliveries. The experience collected in the process enabled us to develop and improve our automatic delivery management systems and eliminate the bottlenecks of the delivery chain, further minimising the costs of servicing single orders.

Miejski Kurier (City Courier) is a same-day-delivery soultion targeting individual and business (e.g. e-commerce) clients in terms of logistics and the final mile deliveries. We believe that the final mile of delivery is often the most important part of the delivery chain, as it involves a pickup from a warehouse and finally timely delivering (with to-the-minute accuracy) to the clients, shops or pickup points.

Our system together with mobile and web apps provides our clients with complete and easy service of the last part of the delivery chain, with an extra focus on timeliness and efficiency of the process.

Foodeli Shop is an ultrafast shopping and delivery service. Its mission is to make the life of people living in big cities more convenient. The mobile app shop offers a range of groceries, food products, stationery, office supplies, medicine and household products - all delivered with lightning speed.

The app is currently operational in several polish cities and growing rapidly. Its functioning is based on mini logistics centers in which the orders are assembled and shipped out. Thanks to innovative logistics and delivery optimisations, Foodeli Shop is able to deliver orders to clients in as little as 20 minutes after ordering.
